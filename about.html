About Us

Over 25 Years...

Colorado Hiking Tours started with a simple idea. To allow people from all walks of life to be able to create memories and experience the amazing wonders of Rocky Mountain National Park in a safe way.

We are a employee owned and operated company that has been in the hiking industry for more than 25 years. We have been been doing guided trips in Rocky Mountain National Park for over 15 of those years. All of our guides are certified in surival tactics as well as
The American Heart Association's Heartsaver ® First Aid CPR AED

Today Colorado Hiking Tours is recognized as one of the leading providers of hiking adventures worldwide, having garnered countless awards including National Geographic Traveler’s "50 Tours of a Lifetime" and Travel + Leisure’s "World’s Best" Awards.
Our main office in located in Estes Park, CO
